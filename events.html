----------Annual Events----------

CHARUSAT EXPO is a vibrant showcase of student innovation and creativity, featuring cutting-edge projects, prototypes, and research across disciplines. It reflects the university’s commitment to excellence, technology, and forward-thinking ideas.

CHARUSAT CONVOCATION celebrates the accomplishments of our graduates, marking the successful completion of their studies. It is a special ceremony where students are honored, inspiring new beginnings and future success.

CHARUSAT SPORAL is the university’s dynamic sports festival, celebrating teamwork, spirit, and athletic excellence. It brings together students across disciplines to compete, collaborate, and showcase their sporting talents in a vibrant and energetic atmosphere.

CHARUSAT VRUND is the university’s vibrant Garba festival, celebrating the rich cultural heritage of Gujarat. It brings students together to experience the joy of traditional dance and music, fostering unity and festive spirit. This colorful event highlights the essence of community, tradition, and celebration in a lively, spirited atmosphere.

----------Technical Events----------

The CHARUSAT X Odoo Event is a collaborative tech initiative bringing together students and industry professionals to explore and innovate using Odoo’s open-source ERP platform. Through workshops, live projects, and interactive sessions, participants gain hands-on experience in business management software, enhancing their skills and industry readiness.

CHARUSAT Pythakon is an exciting coding competition that challenges students to solve real-world problems using Python programming. The event fosters creativity, logical thinking, and technical skills, bringing together aspiring coders to showcase their talent and passion for software development.

CHARUSAT C Quest is a competitive event focused on C programming and problem-solving skills. It encourages students to test their coding abilities, think critically, and engage in a friendly yet challenging environment to sharpen their expertise.

CHARUSAT Coder Arcade is a thrilling coding fest where students compete in diverse programming challenges and hackathons. It’s a platform to showcase coding skills, foster innovation, and collaborate with peers in a vibrant tech environment.
