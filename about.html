About Us

Welcome to the official CHARUSAT Student Portal – your digital companion for academic excellence, collaboration, and innovation. At CHARUSAT, we foster a culture of quality education, research, and community values — all empowered by technology.

This portal brings together all CHARUSAT institutes under one roof — from CSPIT and CMPICA to RPCP and I2IM. Students can effortlessly track results, access learning materials, receive updates on events, and explore placement opportunities — all in one intuitive platform.

With smart features and a student-first approach, we ensure a seamless experience. At CHARUSAT, we don’t just educate minds — we ignite futures.
